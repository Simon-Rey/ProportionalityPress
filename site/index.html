Welcome to the Proportionality Press

The Proportionality Press is a website developed to demonstrate how the principle of proportionality can be applied to improve collective decision-making.

One key example showcased here is the selection of representative comments from online discussions. By applying voting rules that respect diverse perspectives, we can identify a set of comments that best reflect the views of participants — offering a fairer and more informative summary of the conversation.

Explore the articles to see proportionality in action and learn how computational methods can bring clarity and balance to complex discussions.
